## Overview

MODX is a sovereign-grade financial, governance, and security platform designed to operate non-custodial financial rails, synthetic exchange-traded instruments, decentralized governance, and privacy-preserving identity enforcement.

This pre-launch phase confirms the successful deployment and validation of core on-chain infrastructure, including ETF issuance mechanisms, DAO governance foundations, and security enforcement layers.

Experience modules, consumer interfaces, and expanded financial products will activate in post-launch phases.

---

## Scope of Pre-Launch

The following components are active or in final verification during pre-launch:

- Synthetic ETF issuance infrastructure  
- Non-custodial financial rails  
- DAO governance framework (limited enablement)  
- Security and risk containment systems  
- Identity-conditioned access controls  

The following components are **intentionally excluded** from pre-launch and will activate post-launch:

- MODE experience layer  
- MODA experience layer  
- AIRS hybrid services  
- CREATV media layer  
- Full consumer and enterprise interfaces
